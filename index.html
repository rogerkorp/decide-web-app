Instructions

Ratio is an app that helps you find your favorite things

To begin, type in a list of at least three things you would like to choose between. When you're ready, press the Start Vote button to begin the exercise!

During the exercise, you will be given unique pairings of two items in your list. All you have to do is tap on the item you like more!

Once all unique pairings have been voted on, click on the See Results button to see how each list item did! The higher the percentage, the more you liked it!

Please rotate your device

This app is vertical only.
